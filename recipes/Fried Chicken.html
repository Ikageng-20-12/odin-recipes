Ingridients

  • Flour-based batter
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Seasonings such as salt, black pepper, chili powder, paprika, garlic powder

Now since well we have gathered the ingridients, we can get to work, below are the steps you should follow
lets get to chickening!!!!!

Steps

  1. Pour the oil in a skillet
  2. bring it to high heat
  3. Brown the chicken on both sides
  4. then reduce the heat, cover the skillet, and cook for about 30 minutes
  5. Remove the cover, return the heat to high
  6. fry until extra crispy
  7. Drain the chicken on paper towels.01 Mar 2023
